HOVSTAD

[pointing to the door on the right]Go in there for the present.

ASLAKSEN

Yes, our townsfolk have had a very high opinion of you so far, Doctor.

HOVSTAD

[getting up.] Ahem! Doctor, I won't conceal from you the fact—

ASLAKSEN

[hurriedly]Yes, he ran away, Doctor.

HOVSTAD

You must forgive me, Doctor, but sooner or later we must tell you the plain truth— [He is interrupted by the entrance Of MRS. STOCKMANN, who comes in from the street door.]
